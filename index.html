Welcome to Minesweeper!

Game Rules:

1) There are 81 tiles. 10 of them are bombs. Your mission is to clear the area by revealing all of the tiles that are not bombs.

2) You may mark a tile as "safe" by right clicking on it.

3) If you click on a bomb, you lose.

4) If you reveal every tile that is safe, you win!

5) Your score is the number of seconds it took you to clear the area.
